IT Placement Process

Step 1


Student Admission

Enroll in the IT program and complete initial registration to kickstart your placement journey.

Step 2


Placement Orientation

Attend an orientation session to understand the placement process and requirements.

Step 3


Mock Interview

Participate in mock interviews to enhance your confidence and communication skills.

Step 4


Mock Session with Experts

Gain valuable insights from industry experts through detailed mock sessions.

Step 5


Shortlisting

Get shortlisted based on your skills, profile, and mock performance.

Step 6


On Campus Drive

Participate in campus placement drives hosted by reputed companies.

Step 7


Final Interview

Attend the final interview to secure your dream job in IT.

Step 8


Selection

Receive your offer letter and join your new organization.
