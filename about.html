About Us

At BTDC, the Placement Cell is dedicated to guiding students towards successful careers. We ensure that our students are well-prepared for industry demands through various development programs and industry connections. Our team collaborates with top companies such as Google, Amazon, TCS, Microsoft, and Infosys, ensuring our students have access to the best job opportunities.

We focus on enhancing skills and providing real-world experience through mock interviews, workshops, and resume-building sessions. Our aim is not just to place students but also to help them grow into successful professionals.
