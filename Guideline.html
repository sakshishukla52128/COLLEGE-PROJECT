Placement Guidelines for All Streams

Your ultimate guide to acing campus placements, no matter your field!

General Guidelines

  • Prepare an impressive resume tailored to the job role.
  • Work on your communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Keep your LinkedIn profile updated and professional.
  • Participate in mock interviews and practice group discussions.

Stream-Specific Guidelines

Engineering

  • Focus on technical skills such as coding, algorithms, and data structures.
  • Participate in hackathons and coding challenges.
  • Learn relevant tools and technologies (e.g., Git, Python, Java).

Management

  • Build strong analytical and decision-making skills.
  • Prepare for aptitude and case study-based interviews.
  • Gain insights into the latest market trends and industries.

Arts & Humanities

  • Showcase creativity and a strong portfolio of work.
  • Focus on networking and building connections in the industry.
  • Develop skills in research, analysis, and storytelling.

Commerce

  • Gain expertise in financial concepts, accounting, and taxation.
  • Stay updated on economic policies and market trends.
  • Work on Excel and financial modeling tools.
